Our Journey

Initially returning from a pursuit of knowledge and discovery, our founder decided to take his endevaours in Naturopathic Medicine to the next level by applying his garnered wisdoms to the wider community as a means to restore a sense of balance in peoples lives.

Establishing a small clinic in 2007, we begun our ventures as an independant business looking to uplift the local community.

Based in Greater Manchester, situated in the heart of Rochdale at an easy to access location; 5 minutes from the Railway Station, we welcomed people from all backgrounds and beliefs. We still operate from this very location today on weekdays from 9am to 5pm.

Today, 15 years later, we have multiple clinics throughout the country and have recently established a few clinics in Europe and Asia.
