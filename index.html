The Cure Within

Here at Quintessence, we believe in adopting a holistic approach to life - offering a wide range of goods and services - from subtle healing practices that promote well-being to vigourous detoxifications and herbal treatments for a boost in vitality. Inspiring a more natural way of life, we make sure all our products are organically-grown, free from chemical contamination and genetic modification.

Our philosophy is rooted in treating the patient and not in treating the disease. With our dedicated team of healthcare professionals, we look to support the human systems and encourage them to work at their optimum as opposed to suppressing symptoms and working against the body's natural elimination methods.

Catering to the individual, we create a custom revitalisation plan for each of our clients as we consider all living beings as unique creations of God. All our products are organic, pure and ethically sourced. From our Palestinian range in partnership with Zaytoun and LUSH, to our free trade sourcing of products and cruelty-free cosmetics.
